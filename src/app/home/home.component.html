The Adi Dravidar and Tribal welfare Department was set up 1988 after the trifurcation of Social Welfare Deparment, with the objective of providing a more focused approach towards the integrated socio-econimic and educational development of the underprivileged Adi Dravidar and Scheduled Tribles in a co-ordinated and planned manner.

This department is taking sincere efforts to promote education and raise the socio-economic status of the Adi Dravidar and Tribal communities and unlift them by introducing and inplementing various welfare schemes.

Schemes such as, running of Adi Dradivar Welfare schools, hostels, issuing house site pattas and improving basic amenties in Adi Dravidar habitations are being implemented through the commissionerate of Adi Dravidar welfare, for the education socio-economic development of people belonging to Adi Dravidar community.

A State Level Vigilance and Monitoring Committee has been constitute under the chairmanship of the Hon'ble Cheif Minister to review the implementation of scheduled castes and schduled Tribles (Prevention of Atrocities) Act.
